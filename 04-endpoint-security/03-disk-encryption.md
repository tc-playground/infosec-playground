# Disk Encryption

## Introduction

1. `Disk encryption` provides a mechanism for encrypting data on full disks, partitions, removable media, etc.

---

## Operating Systems

1. __Linux__

    1. `DMCrypt`/`LUKS`.

2. __Windows__

    1. `Bit Locker`

    2. `VeraCrypt` - Open source and based on `True Crypt`.

3. __Mac OSX__

    1. `Filevault2`

    2. `VeraCrypt` - Open source and based on `True Crypt`.
